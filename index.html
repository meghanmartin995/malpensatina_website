La Malpensatina

Radda in Chianti, Italy

La Malpensatina is a 400-year old stone farmhouse located outside the hilltown of Radda in Chianti. The farmhouse has stunning views over the surrounding landscape, including its own vineyards and olive groves. The house is approximately one hour south of Florence and half an hour north of Siena, in the heart of Chianti.
