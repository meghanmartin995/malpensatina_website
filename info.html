About Your Stay

The Space

The restored home comfortably sleeps eight adults and two children with five bedrooms and four full baths. On the outside terrace is a swimming pool and children’s playground.

Location

Located just outside of Radda in Chianti, the farmhouse is approximately 1 hour south of Florence, where most guests fly into and rent a car to drive to the house.

Directions

You can always enter “La Malpensatina” on Google Maps and it will route you to the house. If needed, download a pdf of directions.
